Creativity Hub
Unleash your imagination
This Autumn, we are thrilled to launch our new Creativity Hub to nurture and develop creativity. Our remote creative writing course is designed for children and young people aged 11 to 14 years. Class fees for the Creativity Hub and Code Hub
The cost for the term is 60 (apart from Scratch which is free for all). However, places are free for families on benefits, and we offer payment plan options and a bursary fund for discounted places. We believe in making our classes accessible to everyone, and cost should not be a barrier to learning. Please contact us for more information, we are here to help in any way we can.
Why do we charge fees?
As a small charity, your fees help us continue our work and enable us to offer better classes for more children and young people. Thank you for supporting us!

Duke of Edinburghs Award
We are proud to be an Approved Activity Provider for the Skills Section of the Duke of Edinburghs Award, offering Bronze, Silver, and Gold levels remotely.

Students can choose skills to learn from the Code Hub and Creativity Hub. If the Skills Section is longer three months, feel free to learn a different skill each term.
